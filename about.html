Our Story

Welcome to Telly Thabby Sweets Factory, where we have been crafting delicious sweets since 1990. Our journey began with a passion for creating mouthwatering treats that bring joy to people's lives. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved brand known for our commitment to quality and taste.

Our Mission

At Telly Thabby Sweets Factory, our mission is to create delectable sweets that not only satisfy your taste buds but also evoke a sense of happiness and nostalgia. We are dedicated to using the finest ingredients and traditional recipes to ensure that every bite is a delightful experience.

Our Values

  • Quality: We prioritize quality in every aspect of our production process, from sourcing ingredients to packaging our products.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do, and we strive to exceed their expectations with every sweet we create.
  • Innovation: While we cherish our traditional recipes, we also embrace innovation and new flavours to keep our offerings exciting and fresh.
